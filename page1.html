MAP OF KOREA

South Korea is a country in East Asia, constituting the southern part of the Korean Peninsula, and sharing a land border with North Korea. Over 10 million people, around 1/4 of the country's population, live in the Seoul Capital Area. The current density of Seoul is almost twice that of New York City. The land can be divided into four general regions: an eastern region of high mountain ranges and narrow coastal plains; a western region of broad coastal plains, river basins, and rolling hills; a southwestern region of mountains and valleys; and a southeastern region dominated by the broad basin of the Nakdong River.
